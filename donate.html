Lists of Pages to Donate:

1. UN World Food Programme (WFP)

UN World Food Programme (WFP) is currently the largest humanitarian organization that collects money to feed hungry kids and families. WFP has an impact on millions of people worldwide in over 120 countries for over 6 decades, assisting them to survive the effects of conflict, climate change,and poverty. They'd need all available assistance to meet their financing requirement of 22.7 billion USD by 2024 in order to serve 157 million people.

2. Action Against Hunger

Since its founding more than thirty years ago, Action Against Hunger has worked to combat hunger by promoting awareness, providing financial support, introducing clean water and safe sanitation, eventually enhancing food security. They have an influence in over 55+ countries, emphasizing communities that were devastated from natural disasters and conflicts. Through their program, about 21 million people are aided every year.

3. Share The Meal

Under the World Food Programme (WFP), ShareTheMeal, an app was created to make the battle against hunger more accessible. Their team connects people with the desire to help those in need throughout more than 15 countries. On top of that, they provide job opportunities for those who want to contribute to the eradication of world hunger. In their page, you also have the freedom to choose where your donation goes to such as: school meals, sudan families, families in lebanon, or people in palestine.
